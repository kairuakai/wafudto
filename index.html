Sizzling Tofu

Sizzling Tofu is a delicious way to enjoy the bold flavors of sisig in vegetarian form. Made with crisp-fried tofu, chili peppers, and mayonnaise, it’s creamy, spicy, and tasty!

Pork Adobo

Pork Adobo made with succulent pork belly braised in vinegar, soy sauce, garlic, and onions. A delicious balance of salty and savory, this hearty stew is Philippine’s national dish for a good reason!

Sinigang na Baboy

Sinigang na Baboy is the ultimate comfort food! Made with pork ribs, vegetables, and tamarind-flavored broth, it’s hearty and delicious on its own or served with steamed rice.
